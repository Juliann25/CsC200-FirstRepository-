Once upon there lived a lonely polar bear, whose name was Sunny. One day he was really sad, so he sighed a big sigh, which he realized created grass on the ground and winds in the sky. He looked up, astonished, and said to himself, "I wonder what else I could could do." So, he went around exploring to see what else would happen. He found a long log that had two branches on each side and breathed on it. The log started to take a human-like shape as he did, the branches becoming arms and legs. Next, he picked up a couple of rocks and breathed on them, turning them into bugs.
